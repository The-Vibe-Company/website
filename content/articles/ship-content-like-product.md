---
title: Ship content like product
slug: ship-content-like-product
summary: Treat publishing as part of the product surface, not a side process.
publishedAt: 2026-03-02
---

If a piece of content matters, it deserves the same operational standards as product code.

That means:

- versioned source
- reviewable diffs
- reproducible builds
- predictable deploys

Markdown-first publishing is not glamorous, but it is robust. It removes hidden state and makes ownership clear.
